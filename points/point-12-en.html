The palace is designed in close relationship with the square, which enhances its function as an urban and political hub.

Why it is a site of memory

The Palazzo della Libert√† is a place of memory because it materially testifies to the power of the fascist regime and its use of architecture as a propaganda tool. Today it invites reflection on the relationship between architecture, power, and historical memory.
