The project

Itinerari della Memoria 2.0 is a digital project created with the aim of enhancing and making accessible the sites of historical memory present in the area. Through a path that connects physical spaces with digital content, the project allows visitors to rediscover monuments, buildings, and places that in the past were linked to Fascism.

The website presents an itinerary made up of several stops, each identified by a QR code placed at the real location. By scanning the code, users can access information, images, and in-depth materials that help them understand the historical and symbolic value of each site, transforming a simple visit into an experience of informed and conscious learning.
